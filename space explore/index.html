By agreeing to participate in the Mars Expedition Program, all visitors acknowledge the extreme environmental conditions and inherent risks of interplanetary travel. Visitors must comply with safety protocols established by the Mars Habitat Authority, including life-support maintenance, restricted area access, and environmental preservation rules. Any form of contamination—biological or material—is strictly prohibited to protect Mars’ ecosystem and research integrity. The organization holds no liability for unforeseen hazards, including radiation exposure or equipment malfunction. Visitors are expected to act responsibly, follow mission control instructions at all times, and respect the spirit of space exploration for the advancement of humanity
